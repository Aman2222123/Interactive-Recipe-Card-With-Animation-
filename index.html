Recipe Card

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream the sugar and butter together.
  3. Add eggs and beat well.
  4. Mix in the dry ingredients.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes.

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 35 mins

Serving Size: 8 servings
